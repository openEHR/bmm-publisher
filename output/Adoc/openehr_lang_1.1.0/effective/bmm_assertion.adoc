2+^h|*BMM_ASSERTION*

2+a|A statement that asserts the truth of its expression. If the expression evaluates to False the execution generates an exception (depending on run-time settings).

May be rendered in syntax as `assert condition` or similar.

2+|Inherits: xref:LANG:bmm:statement_package.adoc#_bmm_statement_item_class[BMM_STATEMENT_ITEM], xref:LANG:bmm:statement_package.adoc#_bmm_statement_class[BMM_STATEMENT], xref:LANG:bmm:statement_package.adoc#_bmm_simple_statement_class[BMM_SIMPLE_STATEMENT]

2+h|*Attributes*

|*expression*: `xref:LANG:bmm:expression_package.adoc#_el_boolean_expression_class[EL_BOOLEAN_EXPRESSION]` [1..1]
a|Boolean-valued expression of the assertion.

|*tag*: `xref:BASE:foundation_types:primitive_types.adoc#_string_class[String]` [0..1]
a|Optional tag, typically used to designate design intention of the assertion, e.g. `"Inv_all_members_valid"`.
